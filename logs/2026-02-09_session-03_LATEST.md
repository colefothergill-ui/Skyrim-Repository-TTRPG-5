### Narrative Summary

After the successful Goldenglow heist and debriefing with Mercer, Insaerndel returned to the Cistern to rest before meeting Maven Black-Briar. However, the Spell Tome of Quagmire had other plans.

**Rest Compel — Dream-Mirror Descent:**
As Insaerndel settled into his bedroll in the Cistern's quiet corner, the tome's pages began to shimmer with that familiar, unsettling light. The compel was clear: enter Quagmire and face another Mirror Trial, or remain locked out of the tome's deeper secrets. The cost? No stress would clear from this rest.

Insaerndel accepted the compel, earning a Fate point but forgoing the recovery he desperately needed.

**The Quagmire Trial:**
In the dream realm, Insaerndel found himself walking through a distorted reflection of the Cistern. The trial tested his will, his understanding of illusion, and his ability to distinguish truth from Vaermina's manipulations. Through careful navigation of the dream logic and symbolic challenges, he endured the trial.

Upon waking, his mental stress remained (box 1 still marked), but the tome revealed new secrets—progress toward unlocking another spell from its blank pages.

**Social Compel — Informants Everywhere:**
During the evening before rest, Insaerndel had engaged with various Guild members and Riften locals. The GM invoked the "Informants Everywhere" compel from his Trouble aspect: someone in the tavern or market had quietly noted the tall, hooded Altmer's presence and passed word along to interested parties.

Insaerndel accepted this compel as well, earning another Fate point. The consequence: the Thalmor search clock ticked forward. Somewhere in Skyrim, another piece of information about the "Dream Catcher of Dusk" found its way to Dominion ears.

**Sapphire Social Beat:**
Earlier in the evening, before the compels, Insaerndel had approached Sapphire—the Guild's enigmatic member with the guarded demeanor and cutting wit. What started as casual conversation turned into flirtation and social maneuvering.

**Roll: Create Advantage (Deceive +2)** — Success with Style  
Insaerndel's charm and careful words intrigued Sapphire, who rarely shows interest in anyone within the Guild. The success created the aspect **"Sapphire's Intrigue"** with 2 free invokes.

Sapphire's response was measured but clear: she noticed him, and she's curious. This could develop into something more—whether alliance, romance, or complication remains to be seen.

## Scene A1-S12 — Bee and Barb: Maven's Terms

### Scene Overview
**Location:** Riften — Bee and Barb  
**Objective:** Meet Maven Black-Briar and receive terms for the Honningbrew job  
**Outcome:** Success — Terms negotiated, leverage secured, Dampened Spirits quest active

---

### Narrative Summary

With mental stress still weighing on him from the sleepless night in Quagmire, Insaerndel made his way to the Bee and Barb to meet Maven Black-Briar—Riften's shadow matriarch and the true power behind the Thieves Guild's current operations.

Maven sat at her usual corner table, a glass of Black-Briar Reserve in hand, watching the door with the patience of someone who knows everyone comes to her eventually. When Insaerndel approached, she gestured to the seat across from her without preamble.

**Maven's Opening:**
> "You burned exactly three beehives at Goldenglow. Not careless. Not excessive. Precise. Mercer says you're competent. I prefer to verify such claims myself."

The meeting was a test—Maven wanted to see if Insaerndel could operate at her level: disciplined, intelligent, and willing to navigate the complexities of her business without creating mess.

**Negotiation: The Honningbrew Job**

Maven laid out the task:
- **Target:** Honningbrew Meadery in Whiterun, owned by Sabjorn
- **Objective:** Cripple the operation from within
- **Contact:** Mallus Maccius, Maven's plant inside Honningbrew
- **Method:** Secure proof of Sabjorn's vulnerabilities, sabotage production, and collapse his reputation—cleanly

The job would serve dual purposes:
1. Eliminate competition for Black-Briar Meadery
2. Leverage Maven's network connections to trace the Pale-linked shipping note recovered from Goldenglow

**Overcome Challenge: Negotiating from Competence**

Insaerndel recognized this wasn't just a job briefing—it was a power play. Maven was measuring him, deciding whether he was a useful tool or a potential threat to manage.

**Roll: Overcome (Lore +4 via "Eloquent Scholar" stunt) vs. Maven's Deceive (Opposition: Great +4)**
- **Invoked:** "Maven's Message Received" (free invoke from Goldenglow success)
- **Result:** Success with Style (shifted to +6 vs. Maven's +4)

Insaerndel didn't just accept terms—he negotiated them. He referenced the Goldenglow operation, demonstrated understanding of Maven's networks, and positioned himself as an asset worth investing in rather than simply a contractor.

**Boost Gained:** "Silk-Gloved Respect"

Maven's demeanor shifted subtly. She didn't smile—Maven rarely smiles—but her tone changed from evaluative to collaborative.

**Maven's Response:**
> "You understand the game. Good. Complete this job cleanly, and I'll provide more than gold—I'll provide answers. That Pale route you're curious about? I know the names. Deliver results, and we'll discuss them."

**The Terms:**
- **Task:** Travel to Whiterun, contact Mallus Maccius at Honningbrew, secure evidence, sabotage Sabjorn's operation
- **Payment:** To be determined upon completion (Maven's standard practice)
- **Leverage:** Maven promises information about the Pale-linked shipping route—potential lead to Arisann

With the terms agreed upon, Insaerndel accepted the job formally. Maven raised her glass in acknowledgment before returning her attention to her ledger, the meeting concluded.

**Quest Active:** Dampened Spirits
